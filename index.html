Instructions

Click on the buttons to change the facial expression

Click on the eyes to close the eye

Click on the lips to change the lip colour

Click on anywhere else that is not the face to add random coloured circles

Lip colour and eye states are preserved through each expression change

Clicking on lip colour repeatedly will cycle through the lip colours

Clicking on an eye repeatedly will open and close the eye

This assignment works on mobile up to 550px width

This assignment uses no experimental features so is compatible with most browsers

HTML and CSS validated with no issues
